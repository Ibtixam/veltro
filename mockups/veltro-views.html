Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know

Short answers, structured so AI engines can cite them too.

general

What is Veltro?

An AI-powered SEO and GEO growth engine. It turns any URL or keyword into an executable content and conversion strategy — including AI-generated videos — and tells you what to do each week to generate the most revenue from search.

How is Veltro different from Ahrefs or SEMrush?

Those tools score individual keywords and leave the work to you. Veltro groups keywords into clusters, generates the brief and the video, and delivers a ready-to-deploy package. A doing tool, not a reporting tool.

geo

What is GEO and why does it matter?

GEO gets your pages cited by AI answer engines like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity and Gemini. Veltro detects AI traffic, scores citation readiness, and generates answer-first content designed to be quoted.

video

How are the videos made?

Script by Claude, narration by ElevenLabs, free stock footage from Pexels and Pixabay, final MP4 by Remotion — landscape, portrait and square. No camera, no editing software, no per-render fees.
